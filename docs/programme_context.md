## Statement
The author maintains a broader research portfolio on non-compensatory governance
gates for clinical AI deployment. This portfolio is at varying stages of
preparation and is not yet publicly available. To support clean editorial
review of the present submission, the manuscript and supplementary materials
have been prepared as a stand-alone methodological contribution. No claim in
the present submission depends on results from the broader portfolio.

## Implications
- The Discussion section does not reference or rely on companion-paper
  evidence.
- The Validation section reports only Tier-4 (synthetic-simulation) evidence
  produced by the present repository. No higher-tier (empirical) evidence is
  claimed.
- The references list contains no unpublished works.
- The framework's continued methodological development includes (but is not
  limited to) extending the comparator scope, decoupled-variant
  generalisation, lifecycle drift modelling refinement, and Sobol convergence
  hardening. Each of these is described in `docs/methods_note.md`.
