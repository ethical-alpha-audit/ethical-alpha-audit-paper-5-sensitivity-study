Paper 5 Experiment Bundle: Alternative Weighting Sensitivity (A05)
==================================================================

Five compensatory weighting schemes tested via the full P5 SCM pipeline.
NC advantage persists across ALL schemes.

  W0 (baseline 30/20/20/15/15): 152.3x harm ratio
  W1 (equal weights): 191.4x
  W2 (safety-heavy 50%): 50.3x
  W3 (evidence-heavy 35%): 192.8x
  W4 (safety-minimal 10%): 249.2x

Range: 50.3x to 249.2x. Even the most favourable compensatory
configuration produced approximately 50 times more undetected harm.

Reproduction:
  Requires P5 governance friction simulation framework
  N_systems: 10,000 per replicate; N_replicates: 5; Base rate: 0.20
